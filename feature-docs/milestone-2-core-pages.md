## Content Assumptions Used

- Business facts remained based on approved source-of-truth values from Milestone 1.
- Contact details remain placeholders.
- HVAC page was expanded as the deepest service page using the provided HVAC source scope.
- Fire alarm and electromechanical pages were expanded using careful, generic market-appropriate language.

## Claims Intentionally Avoided

Milestone 2 intentionally does **not** claim:

- authority approvals
- ADCD/Civil Defence approvals
- certifications (ISO or otherwise)
- licensed contractor status
- fake project counts, client names, testimonials, or awards

## SEO and Linking Improvements

- Unique page titles and meta descriptions updated for the expanded pages.
- Stronger heading hierarchy and section-level keyword relevance added.
- Contextual internal links added via service cards, CTA blocks, and related-services strips.
- Service pages now cross-link to each other and to `/contact-us` and `/services`.
